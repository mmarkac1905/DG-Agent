# Seed Taxonomy (generated)

One row per seed in `dbt/seeds/`: category and purpose. Generated from `seed_taxonomy.csv` by `build_knowledge_wiki.py`; drift from the CSVs on disk fails `tests/test_seed_taxonomy_invariant.py`.
